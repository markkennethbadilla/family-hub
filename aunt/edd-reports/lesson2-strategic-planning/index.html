Defining Organizational Purpose

709 · Chapter 44.1 Mission

Defining the constructWhat a mission statement is

Mission statementA concise declaration of an organization's core purpose and reason for existing — what it does, for whom, and to what end.
In plain terms: one or two sentences saying why we exist.

709 · Chapter 44.1 Mission

A worked example

Sample school mission"To give every child a safe place to learn the skills and values they need for life."

Who: every child · What: skills and values · Why: a good life. Short, clear, memorable.

Establishing KPIs

709 · Chapter 44.3 KPIs

Defining the constructThe scoreboard for strategy

KPIKey Performance Indicator — a quantifiable measure used to evaluate whether a goal is being met.
In plain terms: a number that tells us if we are winning.
709 · Chapter 44.3 KPIs

What KPIs look like

  • Pass rateThe share of students who passed.
  • Monthly attendanceTracked over time to spot trends.
  • Teachers trainedProgress on capacity building.
  • Dropout rateWatched closely, aiming downward.
