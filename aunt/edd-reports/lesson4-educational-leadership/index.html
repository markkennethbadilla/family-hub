Values & Ethics in Educational Administration

ED.D. 703 · Chapter 1111.1 Values & ethics

Two words, clearly definedValues vs. ethics

ValuesThe beliefs we hold about what is important — honesty, fairness, respect, service.
In plain terms: what we care about most.
EthicsThe standards of right and wrong that guide how we act on those values.
In plain terms: doing the right thing, especially when it is hard.
ED.D. 703 · Chapter 1111.1 Values & ethics

Why this matters in administration

  • Decisions affect many livesHiring, budgets, discipline — each touches students and staff.
  • Leaders set the toneA school takes on the character of the person who leads it.
  • Trust is the foundationWithout ethical conduct, no policy can hold.

Why it works
