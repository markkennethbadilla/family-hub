EdD 714 · Chapter 55.4 Statistics

What statistics are for

Statistics help us decide whether a result is genuine — or whether it could have happened by luck. You choose the right tool; you do not memorize the math.
EdD 714 · Chapter 55.4 Statistics

Common tools — and when to use each

  • t-testCompares the averages of two groups.
  • ANOVACompares three or more groups at once.
  • RegressionExamines how variables are related.
  • Chi-squareTests patterns in counts and categories.
